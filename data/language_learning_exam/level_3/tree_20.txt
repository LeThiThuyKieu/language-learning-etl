NODE MATCHING

1. pull (a few) strings -> use your influence in order to get something you want or to help someone, especially when this is unfair
2. opt out (of) -> decide not to take part in something or stop taking part in it
3. not have a leg to stand on -> not have any way of proving that you are right about something
4. It is only -> a matter of time before the situation becomes uncontrollable.
5. are being gradually -> restored
6. There is also a spacious and comfortable family room, which should appeal to diners with young children. -> Restaurant
7. mean -> business
8. fall short -> not reach a particular level or to fail to achieve something you were trying to do
9. a stitch in time (saves nine) -> used for saying that it is better to solve a problem now, rather than leave it until later when it may be more difficult to deal with
10. a fish out of water -> sb who feels uncomfortable in unfamiliar surroundings
