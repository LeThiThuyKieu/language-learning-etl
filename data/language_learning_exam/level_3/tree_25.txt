NODE MATCHING

1. crowd around -> move to a particular place at the same time as a lot of other people
2. get sth off your chest -> talk to someone about something that has been worrying you, so that you feel better about it
3. press ahead/on (with) -> continue doing something in a determined way, despite difficulties, opposition or interruptions
4. pass on -> give someone something, for example a message, that someone else has given you
5. pride of place -> in the place that is most central or important
6. knee-high to a grasshopper -> very small, because you were very young
7. stop dead in your tracks -> suddenly stop, for example because you are surprised
8. touch wood -> used when you have said that you have had good luck in order to prevent bad luck from happening to you
9. cool down -> become cooler, or make something cooler
10. talk down to -> talk to someone as if you think they are not as clever or important as you are
