NODE MATCHING

1. down on your luck -> having a long period of difficulty finding work and earning money
2. on offer -> available
3. wear down -> make something gradually disappear or become thinner by using or rubbing it; make someone gradually lose their energy or confidence
4. dig up -> remove something from under the ground by digging; dig holes in an area of land; find information by searching carefully
5. embezzlement -> stealing money from the company where you work
6. satellite town -> town or city that is close to and depends on a larger city
7. shout down -> make it difficult to hear what someone says by shouting while they are speaking
8. add fuel to the fire -> make a bad situation worse
9. warm up -> prepare for a sport or activity by doing gentle exercises or practising just before it starts warm-up (n)
10. six of one (and) half a dozen of the other -> used for saying that two things are equally good or bad
