NODE LISTENING

Q1: Shuttles were originally passed back and forth by ___ .
Answer: hand

Q2: Saltwater crocodiles ___ in the wet season , when water levels are at their highest .
Answer: mate

Q3: The greater roadrunner nests on a platform of ___ low in a cactus or a bush and lays three to six eggs , which hatch in 20 days .
Answer: sticks

Q4: The idiom is based on the honor shown to ___ in Hinduism .
Answer: cows

Q5: Stotting makes a prey animal more ___ , and uses up time and energy that could be spent on escaping from the predator .
Answer: visible

Q6: The least skipper can be ___ in damp or wet habitats with tall grasses .
Answer: found

Q7: When writing ___ amounts the location of the symbol varies by currency .
Answer: currency

Q8: The tower is situated at the ___ side of the place , where the land walls of the city join the sea walls .
Answer: southern

Q9: In the Gregorian calendar , 303 out of every 400 ___ are common years .
Answer: years

Q10: There are two ___ of the anthem : a long and a short one .
Answer: versions
