1. give off -> produce sth such as heat or a smell

4. appearance -> the way that someone or something looks
5. see off -> go to a train station, etc, to see someone leave
6. live -> to have your home in a particular place
7. show off -> try to attract people's attention and make them admire you (usually used negatively)
8. Mr Greene seems to find it difficult to get -> on with people from other countries.
